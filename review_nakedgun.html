Naked Gun

Naked Gun: From the Files of Police Squad! is a hilarious and entertaining movie that is sure to leave you laughing out loud. Leslie Nielsen's performance as the bumbling detective Frank Drebin is brilliant and the comedic timing is impeccable. The film's clever writing and absurd gags make for a fun and memorable viewing experience. Overall, if you're looking for a lighthearted comedy that doesn't take itself too seriously, Naked Gun: From the Files of Police Squad! is definitely worth checking out.
